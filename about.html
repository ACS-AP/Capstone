Why Us

At GreenPrint, we have a very rich value proposition. Aside from merely displaying someone's usage data, we also want to put that data into context. This is vital becuase if the app said your houshold used 60 gallons of water today, you would have no idea weather that's good or bad. In America, the average person uses 90+ gallons of water per day so that means 60 is actually fantastic. Meanwhile, in sub Saharan Africa, the average person consumes a mere 3-5 gallons of water per day. I think it is imperative that people understand just how much we use, especially considering an average US resident is the biggest culprit of overuse in the world. Although we have a long way to go, GreenPrint can become that first step in the right direction through increasing awarness.

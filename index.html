GreenPrint

Display

GreenPrint is an application that allows users to view their household consumption. This means a user can view their water, gas and electric usage on a given month or year. This technology draws already availiable data from providers and boldy displays it for its users.

Impact

The impact tracker puts your usage in visual terms. For instance, your usage could have filled up 20 swimming pools, killed 100 trees and so forth. This feature aims to have a similiar impact as the comparison.

Comparison

The second most important feature in GreenPrint, allowing you to compare yourself to your neighborhood, state, country and worldwide. This has the ability to shed light on the excess an average American uses.
